TOZ 23F – Portal Notification I would like to suggest adding a chat notification indicating which player pressed “Up” on the portal. This would improve transparency within the party, as it allows players to clearly see which party member triggered the portal prematurely. Aran – Combo Tempest in TOZ On certain TOZ stages, Combo Tempest can fully kill mobs, but on other stages (such as 8F, 11F, and 16F) the skill only reduces mobs to 1 HP, similar to Heaven’s Hammer. Could this be reviewed for consistency across all stages, allowing Combo Tempest to eliminate mobs in the same way it does on other floors? Ranged Classes – Close Range Attack Interaction in TOZ For ranged classes such as Night Lord, Night Walker, Marksman, and Bowmaster, there are certain stages where players can stand very close to mobs and still attack normally. However, on some stages (for example 30F with the Lion charge mechanic), ranged attackers are unable to attack when the boss is too close and must maintain a certain distance. The lion charges like a mad mob constantly and this change could aid the mentioned jobs to constantly attacking instead of playing hide & seek with the lion while other jobs constantly attack. Would it be possible to allow ranged attackers to continue attacking even when the boss is in close proximity, similar to how other stages currently function? This would improve consistency across TOZ mechanics for ranged classes. TOZ NPC in the Gacha Room Would it be possible to duplicate the TOZ NPC into the Free Market Gacha room? Currently, when players want to liquidate their Inkwell and Oz coins into White Scrolls, they need to travel between the TOZ lobby and the FM Gacha room. This process becomes repetitive when doing it across multiple characters. Having the NPC in the Gacha room would make the process much more convenient to liquidate both coins into WS. Dual Blade – Final Cut Passive Owl Effect The owl animation effect above the Dual Blade/Blade Master character from the Final Cut passive animation can sometimes block visibility of boss mechanics. For example, it may obscure Damage Reflect indicators on shorter bosses such as, TOZ 10F Spider and Ravana (Body 1). When a Dual Blade is standing directly on the boss while melee attacking, other players may have difficulty seeing these indicators. Would it be possible to toggle or adjust the opacity of this effect, similar to how summons like Bahamut allow opacity adjustments? Easier Pet Equipment Management Many players have multiple characters and frequently transfer tradeable pet equipment between them. Equipping pet gear can feel tedious since it requires, Double-clicking the item, Selecting the specific pet by clicking next pet, then equipping them, then repeating the process for up to three pets. Perhaps a simplified system or quick-equip option could be introduced to make equipping pet equipment less cumbersome. Static GM Events The static fixed GM events currently seem to have very little participation. It may help to review or improve the rewards to make them more attractive. Similar to how GM-hosted events often draw large numbers of players, better incentives could encourage more participation in these scheduled events and potentially increase the number of players logging in during those fixed timings.
