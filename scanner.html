How it works

1. Press Start Listening.
2. Hold your device near the in-game BGM.
3. The scanner sends 1.5s audio chunks to the server and tries to match the music.

Best supported on desktop Chrome right now.
Mobile Safari / some browsers may not support the required audio format yet.

Controls
Idle. Press "Start Listening" to begin.
Result
No detection yet.

Once a strong match is found, scanning will automatically stop and show the map name.
